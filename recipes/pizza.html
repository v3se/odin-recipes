Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This recipe is for making delicious homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured surface until it is the size of your pizza pan.
  3. Transfer the dough to the pizza pan.
  4. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, parmesan cheese, and basil.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown, about 15 minutes.
